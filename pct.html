Pre-conference tour

Discover Sarajevo

Before we kick off the 51st CEC, we’re taking you on a journey to the heart of Bosnia – Sarajevo, a city where East meets West, and history, culture, and people intertwine like nowhere else.

Wander through the lively streets of Baščaršija, visit the iconic Sebilj fountain, and take in the unique blend of Ottoman, Austro-Hungarian, and modern influences that shape Sarajevo’s identity.

Enjoy a relaxed afternoon exploring local shops, tasting traditional food, and soaking in the atmosphere of a place where three religions and cultures have lived side by side for centuries.

✨ Don’t miss this opportunity to experience Sarajevo’s charm with your fellow participants!
