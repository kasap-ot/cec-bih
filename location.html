Hotel Zenica, Zenica, Bosnia and Herzegovina

About Zenica

Hotel Zenica is part of Kamberovića Polje, a green oasis featuring walking trails, athletic facilities (stadium, tennis, football, track), and adjacent to a shopping mall and fairgrounds—ideal for leisure and convenience. The city itself lies along the scenic Bosna River, surrounded by hills—striking yet intimate.

Getting There from Sarajevo

Travel Costs

  • By Bus: ~14 € (round trip)
  • By Car: ~20 € (fuel + toll) – fuel price around 1.3 €/L
  • By Train: 5–7 € one way

Train Times

From Sarajevo to Zenica

  • Train 2150: departs at 04:44, arrives at 06:28
  • Train 2156: departs at 15:41, arrives at 17:25

Return from Zenica to Sarajevo

  • Train 2151: departs at 04:59, arrives at 06:39
  • Train 2157: departs at 15:32, arrives at 17:42

The journey takes approximately 1 hour and 39–40 minutes.
